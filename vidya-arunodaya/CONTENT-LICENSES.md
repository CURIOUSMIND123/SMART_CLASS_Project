# Content licences — Vidya Arunodaya offline pack

Every bundled asset is included legally, with attribution preserved in the app.

| Asset | Source | Licence | How it's used |
|---|---|---|---|
| Interactive simulations (English + Hindi) | PhET Interactive Simulations, University of Colorado Boulder — phet.colorado.edu | **CC BY 4.0** — redistribution (incl. offline & commercial) allowed with attribution | Bundled offline in `sims/`, attribution shown on every simulation |
| NCERT chapter text | ncert.nic.in / diksha.gov.in | CC BY-NC-ND (textbooks) | **Not copied.** App links out to the official chapter (opens when online). Our in-app notes are original summaries. |
| 3D models | Khronos glTF Sample Models | Royalty-free samples | Shown only to demonstrate the offline 3D viewer; production replaces with licensed/CC educational models |
| Original lessons (Human Eye, PlantVerse) | Built for this programme | Programme-owned | Full offline rights |
| UI local-language strings (Nyishi) | Written for this pack | Indicative sample | Demonstrates localisation; production strings validated with SCERT/local educators |

## Principles
- We **never** scrape, copy, modify, re-host or commercially repackage NCERT/DIKSHA content — we link to the official source.
- PhET content keeps its required attribution: *"PhET Interactive Simulations, University of Colorado Boulder, https://phet.colorado.edu."*
- The programme charges for **software, curation, integration, localisation and service** — never for the free/official content itself.
- Any premium third-party library (e.g. iDream) would be added only under a signed per-device licence.
